## Variant A — Minimal statement

This programme requests no access to any national facility listed by the Engineering and Physical Sciences Research Council (EPSRC). All experimental and computational infrastructure required to deliver the work packages exists within the UCL Advanced Propulsion Laboratory (APL) and is described in the Research Environment sub-section of the Approach and in the Resources and Cost Justification section. Grant funds procure project-specific equipment only; no national facility time is sought.
